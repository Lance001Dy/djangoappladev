About

This is an online blog application for ProCure Solutions (Pty) Ltd. The application allows users to register to the platform, create a profile, create, update and delete blog posts, and view a dashboard of posts from registered users on the Home page.The user may filter through user posts by selecting the respective author of a particular post.
